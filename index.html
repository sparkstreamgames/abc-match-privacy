Information Collected by Third-Party Services

Advertising Services (Google AdMob)

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in our App. AdMob may automatically collect certain information for advertising purposes, including:

  • Device type and model

  • IP address (which may indicate general location)
  • App usage data and interaction with ads

Important: We have configured AdMob to serve only child-directed, non-personalized advertisements in compliance with COPPA. This means AdMob does not use persistent identifiers to track children across apps or websites for behavioral advertising.

For more information about Google's privacy practices, please visit:

  • Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google AdMob Privacy Information: https://support.google.com/admob/answer/6128543

How Information Is Used

The limited device information collected by AdMob is used solely for:

  • Displaying advertisements within the App
  • Measuring ad performance and detecting fraud
  • Providing core App functionality

We do not use any information for purposes beyond ad serving and App operation.

Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

ABC MATCH is designed for children and complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Our Commitment to Children's Privacy:

  • We do not require children to provide any personal information to use the App
  • We use only child-directed, non-personalized advertising
  • We do not share children's information with third parties except as necessary to provide the App service (displaying ads)
  • Parents can review our privacy practices at any time

Parental Controls

Parents can:

  • Review this Privacy Policy at any time
  • Contact us with questions about our privacy practices
  • Request information about the types of data collected through AdMob

On iOS devices, parents can limit ad tracking by:

  1. Going to Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking
  2. Disabling "Allow Apps to Request to Track"

App Tracking Transparency (iOS)

When you first open ABC MATCH on an iOS device, you will see a prompt asking for permission to track your activity. This tracking is used solely for personalized advertising. You may choose to allow or deny this permission. Your choice does not affect the core functionality of the App, but ads may be less relevant if you deny tracking permission.
